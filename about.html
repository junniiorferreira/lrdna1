DNA1

DNA1 is the new consumer assurance product from Lloyd’s Register (LR), it combines the very latest approach in NGS DNA testing to confirm a product’s contents, origins and composition, together with an audit of the manufacturer’s processes to ensure that the product you are holding is from the same batch that has been tested.

This means DNA1 gives you a higher level of confidence in what you eat-assurance like never before!

DNA1 Assurance also covers Non-GMO and Gluten Free. NGS DNA testing is supplied by Clear Labs Inc.

Clear Labs’ Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) DNA testing is the basis of LR’s DNA1 food assurance schemes for 100% Authentic, Non-GMO and Gluten-Free foods.

Better Authenticity Assurance

Clear Labs’ delivers high-resolution, universal NGS DNA tests that cover every potential biological adulterant. Powering stronger food safety systems and guarantee ingredient and product integrity.

Better Non-GMO Assurance

One Clear Labs NGS DNA test gives all possible GMO sequences within a sample. This scientifically guarantees that a product is in fact GMO-free through NGS technology.
